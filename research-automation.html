HybrohtResearcher/1.0 (+https://hybroht.com/research-automation; contact@hybroht.com)

Purpose and Goals

HybrohtResearcher is a cluster of different tools designed for research purposes.
Its main goal is to automate the discovery of relevant content for our projects.
Secondary objectives include connecting our utilities with other platforms, such as providing thumbnail previews or enabling indexing of websites in our internal search engine.


We may access your platforms to:

  • Analyze content for relevance within specific fields (e.g., academic trends).
  • Generate thumbnail previews or one-sentence calls for pages on your website. (e.g., Bluesky posts).
  • Monitoring tasks, checking your platform's availability, mostly on behalf of our partners.
  • Index your website in a search engine.

We do not:

  • Harvest your website's data to sell it.
  • Collect website data to train generative models.
  • Ignore robots.txt directives when they are applicable.
  • Ignore opt-out requests.

Security Measures

  • Static IP addresses associated with hybroht.com subdomains.
  • Operation schedule, rate limiting, and related measures to prevent excessive access (e.g., a high number of requests per second).
  • Programming methods combined with LLM-based interpretation of robots.txt for dynamic compliance.

Capabilities

  • Allowlists and blocklists to honor opt-out requests and avoid undesired content in our research.
  • Fetching website content preferably via official APIs or RSS feeds. HybrohtResearcher can perform GET requests on pre-specified web links for content analysis but will not follow new links that appear in a response.
  • Our tools check a platform's robots.txt file before fetching any content. We use a combination of parsing techniques and large language model (LLM) tools to ensure full compliance, even if your robots.txt is poorly formatted or contains extra specifications.
  • Re-checks the robots.txt file for updates every 3 days.

Limitations

  • As a cluster of tools, it does not track whether it has accessed your website earlier the same day during other tasks. Therefore, it may access the same URL again in a different task.
  • Our tools respect restrictions: if they are blocked by your platform, they will move on and will not attempt to circumvent the block.
  • The tools do not verify robots.txt when calling methods that only fetch metadata about a resource, such as HTTP HEAD requests.
  • As of this date, the information provided on this page is available only in English.

Technical Details

The tools operate using workflow orchestration in a Linux environment. They interact with target systems primarily through APIs and RSS feeds.

Data Sources

The tools primarily collect data from knowledge bases and news platforms. Occasionally, they may also gather relevant public social media posts for ongoing research. Collected data is restricted to news-related content suitable for our research projects, such as breaking news. Our tools do not directly collect or focus on personal user data.

Operation Schedule

The tools operate on a daily schedule, preferably during regular business hours, but they may also run outside these times.


Access and Usage Policies

HybrohtResearcher is programmed to honor robots.txt directives, even if the file is incorrectly formatted or contains unsupported rules. The preferred methods for fetching content are through APIs or RSS feeds; however, HTTP GET requests to website URLs may occur for certain tasks.
If content retrieval fails after one attempt, our tools will not retry to conserve resources and respect your site preferences. Opt-out requests from website owners will be fully respected without requiring a reason.

Privacy and Compliance

No part of HybrohtResearcher is designed to actively collect personal user data. Any incidental data of this kind is neither targeted nor retained; it is promptly discarded or anonymized to mitigate security and legal risks.
While our separate business partnerships may require handling their sensitive data, such projects are not directly involved in our own research process.

Opt-Out Mechanism

If you wish to opt out of being researched by this system, please email us at contact@hybroht.com with your request.
Include "[Opt-Out]" at the beginning of the subject line and add your website in the email body.
Optionally, you may also provide a reason.

Please note: although providing a motive is optional, any eventual opt-back will not be allowed without a reason. For operational safety, entries in our domain blocklists will not be removed if their registry lacks a motive.


Troubleshooting and Support

For any issues or concerns related to HybrohtResearcher (not including the opt-out mechanism described above), please contact us through one of our channels at https://hybroht.com/contact.
